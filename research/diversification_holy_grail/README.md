# Diversification — The Holy Grail of Investing

Based on Ray Dalio's insight that combining uncorrelated return streams dramatically reduces portfolio risk while maintaining returns.

Simulates portfolios of 1-20 assets at varying correlation levels (0% to 70%) and shows that uncorrelated streams can halve risk with just 6-7 assets, while highly correlated streams offer minimal benefit beyond 3-4.

## References

- [Ray Dalio: Holy Grail of Investing](https://www.investopedia.com/video/play/ray-dalio-his-portfolio-holy-grail/)
- *Principles: Life and Work* — Ray Dalio
